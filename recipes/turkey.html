Turkey

Description

Thanksgiving has never been easier than now!

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Turkey
  • Butter (lots of butter)

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 450 degrees
  2. Cover Turkey in Butter
  3. Place Turkey in preheated oven
  4. Once Turkey starts to brown, reduce oven to 250
  5. Take Turkey out before it burns, but after it's fully cooked
