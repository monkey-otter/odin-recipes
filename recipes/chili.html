Chili

Description

It's better than the canned stuff at least...

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of beans
  • 2 pounds of ground beef
  • 4 cups chicken stock
  • whatever seasonings you have laying around

Steps

  1. Rinse beans in water
  2. Soak beans in water for at least 4hrs
  3. in a Dutch Oven, brown Ground Beef
  4. add beans to ground beef
  5. Add whatever seasonings feel right in your heart
  6. Add Chicken Stock
  7. Place lid on dutch oven, cook on low for at least 4 hours, stiring occaisionally
